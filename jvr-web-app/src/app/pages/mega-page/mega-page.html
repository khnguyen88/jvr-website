Embedded. Accountable. Unified.

Jay founded JvR on the belief that the best outcomes happen when the boundary between client and consultant disappears. When we take on an engagement, we are not a separate vendor managing a ticket queue — we are part of your team, sharing the same goals, the same accountability, and the same definition of success.

Integration Approach

Working Within Your Team

When you have a strong internal team but need additional skills or capacity, we embed directly into your workflow. We adopt your tools, attend your standups, and contribute as a natural extension of your existing group.

Full Ownership Approach

Owning the Full Process

When you need a team to drive an initiative end-to-end, we take full ownership — from discovery and planning through implementation, QA, and post-launch support — while keeping your stakeholders informed and involved throughout.

A Community Worth Investing In

Grand Rapids drew Jay in for a few reasons: the variety of strong industries here, a tech community that genuinely supports one another, and a regional culture that values doing good work over putting on a show. We are proud to be part of it, and we try to give back where we can.

Beer City Code

Jay is an organizer for one of the Midwest's premier developer conferences — 37 speakers, approximately 500 attendees, and two days of world-class technical content in Grand Rapids.

West Michigan Azure Users Group

Monthly meetups where local practitioners share knowledge, tackle real problems, and support each other's growth in Microsoft's cloud platform.

Hour of Code

Jay has presented at area schools through the Hour of Code initiative, helping introduce the next generation to software development and the opportunities it can create.
