Where We Came From
"The client has the vision. I am their trusted partner who will make that vision a reality." — Jay von Rosen, Founder

After years at software firms that gradually shifted their focus from solving real problems to building their own reputations, Jay wanted something different — a place where the client's vision comes first, developers are valued for their craft, and the work speaks for itself.

The company name is a fitting reflection of that mindset. Jay spent weeks searching for the perfect name, came up empty, and eventually just used his initials. It is practical, unpretentious, and to the point — and that is still how we approach everything.

A Community Worth Investing In

Grand Rapids drew Jay in for a few reasons: the variety of strong industries here, a tech community that genuinely supports one another, and a regional culture that values doing good work over putting on a show. We are proud to be part of it, and we try to give back where we can.

Beer City Code

Jay is an organizer for one of the Midwest's premier developer conferences — 37 speakers, approximately 500 attendees, and two days of world-class technical content in Grand Rapids.

West Michigan Azure Users Group

Monthly meetups where local practitioners share knowledge, tackle real problems, and support each other's growth in Microsoft's cloud platform.

Hour of Code

Jay has presented at area schools through the Hour of Code initiative, helping introduce the next generation to software development and the opportunities it can create.
